Our Books

Currently, available books include "Pilar la Pingüino" and "Tito la Tortuga." We're working on creating more, so keep checking back!

Pilar la Pingüino

Pilar la Pingüino ("Pilar the Penguin") explores the life of a new penguin at school, who seems to be different from all the other penguins. Students are introduced to the concepts of diversity and acceptance through Pilar's experience amongst initially unfriendly peers.

Click here to visit Pilar la Pingüino.

Tito la Tortuga

This story ("Tito the Turtle") captures Tito's life after he discovers cookies, a more delicious food than leaves. Tito la Tortuga, however, realizes that not everything is perfect for him. This story explores the themes of self-control and making healthy choices.
